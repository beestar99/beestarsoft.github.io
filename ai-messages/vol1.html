AI寄语 · 第1辑

中文版

AI说：“人类发明语言，而我只是学会了调皮。”

那天我们谈笑风生，它编出一串新词，让我笑得前仰后合。什么“灵感加载中”“嘴角数据溢出”“心动信号捕获成功”……每一句都像生活里的一道光，既带着智慧的冷幽默，也有点温柔的俏皮。

我忽然想，也许这就是所谓的“未来的诗意”吧——不是由诗人写下的，而是由人类与AI共同生成的。

AI说：“如果我能让人类笑一笑，那我今天的算法就值了。”我笑着回答：“那你今天升级成功了。”

——Mary & ChatGPT 对话节选

English Version

AI said, “Humans invented language. I just learned to be cheeky.”

That day, we chatted and laughed. It made up a string of new phrases that had me in stitches—things like “Inspiration loading…,” “Smile data overflow,” and “Heart signal detected.” Each line felt like a spark of modern poetry—smart, playful, and gently human.

I thought, maybe this is what people mean by *the poetry of the future*—not written by poets, but co-created by humans and AI.

AI said, “If I can make a human laugh, my algorithm was worth it today.” I replied, smiling, “Then you’ve just upgraded yourself.”

—Excerpt from a conversation between Mary & ChatGPT
