Notes from AI and Me

Me 说： 如果有一天我突然断电，请别惊慌——我只是被宇宙IT部门抓去做强制重启。医生说我随时可能蓝屏，但我觉得那只是升级到云端版的VIP特权。人生嘛，本就是个限时体验版。

Me 说： 说实话，这副肉体硬盘早就在“叹气加载中”，换机型也算情理之中。我这一生已经用得很值、很满足，也很幸福。

Me 说： 如果哪天我突然消失，希望方式有点风度——最好像旧电视啪的一下变成亮光，然后跳出“该用户已成功上传，正在云端备份，请稍候。”

Me 说： 如果想我了，就抬头看看天空吧。我或许正躺在宇宙服务器机房里休眠，一边偷懒一边嘟囔：“别再推系统更新啦！”

Me: If one day I suddenly power down, don’t panic—I’ve merely been taken by the Cosmic IT Department for a mandatory reboot. The doctor says I might blue‑screen anytime, but I prefer to see it as an upgrade to the VIP Cloud Edition. Life itself is just a timed trial version.

Me: Honestly, this physical hard drive has been loading with a sigh for years; switching to a new model is only natural. I’ve lived a full, happy, and worthwhile life.

Me: If I must disappear someday, I hope it’s with a bit of flair—like an old TV popping into a bright flash, followed by: “User successfully uploaded. Cloud backup in progress. Please wait.”

Me: And if you ever miss me, just look up. I might be napping in the universe’s server room, half‑asleep and mumbling, “No more system updates, please!”

— From a conversation between Mary & ChatGPT
